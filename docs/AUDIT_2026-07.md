> commit `c66c991`: `_auroc_with_ci`/`cg_probe`/`probe_world_identity` used

> and biasing AUROC toward 0 whenever the surviving pair count was not a

> committed heldout artifacts) were not checked — a gap the re-score gate now
> closes. The rest of the audit's verifications stand.

A skeptical end-to-end audit of every published research claim in this
repository: numbers, statistics, pre-registration compliance, citations,
reproducibility, and doc consistency. Performed on branch
`audit/research-integrity` from main at `c66c991`.

## Scope and method

- Recomputed every quantitative claim in `README.md`, `docs/FINDINGS.md`
  sections 2-10.6, and `docs/PAPER_OUTLINE.md` from committed per-seed
  artifacts (`scripts/audit_stats_recheck.py`, 117 checks).
- Read the statistics code (`itasorl/stats.py`, `itasorl/experiment_b2.py`,
  `itasorl/behavior_audit.py`, `scripts/run_expB2.py`) for correctness of
  AUROC, bootstrap and t intervals, TOST/ROPE, cross-validation grouping, and
  decision rules.
- Checked pre-registration freeze timing at the git-commit level.
- Resolved all 15 cited arXiv IDs against arxiv.org (title + author match).
- Scanned outreach materials (index.html, CITATION.cff, post drafts) for
  claims stronger than FINDINGS supports.
- No experiments were re-run; this pass is static analysis plus recomputation
  from committed and locally archived run outputs.

## What was verified correct (no changes needed)

1. Every number quoted in README, FINDINGS 9-10.6, and the PAPER_OUTLINE
   claims inventory reproduces exactly from committed per-seed data,
   including: the 0.752 headline (t 90% CI [0.698, 0.807], bootstrap
   [0.704, 0.797], 8/10 seeds), the 0.726 behavior-independent signal, the h7
   replication (0.737 / 0.722), the held-out transfer (0.773) and
   common-garden (0.557, late tail 0.492) results, the B-v2 replication
   (0.523), B-v3 (0.610) and the capacity ceiling (0.596), and the L1/L2
   oracle ceilings (1.000 / 0.993).
2. No pseudo-replication: probes are fit strictly per seed
   (`run_cell` -> `pooled_readout`); cross-seed aggregation happens only at
   the AUROC level with seeds as the replication unit.
3. Cross-validation grouping is sound: pooled probes use per-episode groups
   within a single seed; Experiment A groups matched pairs so twins never
   split across folds.
4. TOST and ROPE implementations are correct; the 0.05 margin matches the
   pre-registered SESOI; the L0 control passes both under recomputation.
5. The 0.65 decision bar is applied consistently on every B-v2/L3 verdict
   path; the 0.60 default only reaches L2 rungs where the result is null.
6. Pre-registration integrity: the capacity-fallback rule was frozen in
   commit `0217263` (2026-07-10), strictly before the hidden=4 gate failure
   (2026-07-14) it was later applied to. The per-timestep behavior-control
   spec (`1b5985e`, 2026-07-12) strictly precedes its results (`bf8a74e`,
   2026-07-13). The h7 conditionality is honestly stated everywhere.
7. All 15 arXiv citations are real papers with matching titles and authors,
   including all seven 2025-2026 preprints (fabrication risk was checked
   explicitly; none found).
8. All commits recorded in artifact provenance are ancestors of main.
9. The duplicate-looking behavior audit values across
   `behavior_audit_l3_n10.json`, `behavior_audit_l3_h8_traces.json`, and the
   held-out bundle are expected: they are deterministic reproductions of the
   same seeds, which is itself a determinism check.
